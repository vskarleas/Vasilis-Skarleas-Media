Description

This is the story of 11 people that came close together, and they experienced some great moments at Paros Island in 2021. Summer vibes are loaded after this trip and it stays in our hearts for many different reasons (personals included). Hope you enjoy!

Drone footage quality could be better but I didn't downloaded directly from SD card, but from cloud instead. Custom LUTs are used for color correction. The final size of the video is 3.72Gb. It took 10 hours to edit and collect the media sources. The rendering and sharing process took about 3 hours. The whole editing file size is 101.27Gb.

In this video the music is copyrighted and that's why the video will set as private or unlisted after premier has ended. Songs are selected according to everyone's hearings and preferences as well as based on video requirements. This video is a composition of three different videos divided into the three full days of the trip.
